FAQ

Questions?

Do I need to buy any equipment?

No. Everything is included — tub, chiller, delivery, and ongoing maintenance.

Do I need to add ice?

No. The chiller keeps the water at the right temperature automatically.

What do I need at home?

An outdoor power socket, an outdoor tap, and a level outdoor space for the tub.

What areas do you cover?

Surrey and surrounding areas including Horley, Crawley, and Reigate.

What if something breaks?

We take care of it. Repairs and replacements are covered as part of your rental.

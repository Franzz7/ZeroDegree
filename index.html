Everything You Need to Know

Your questions, answered.

Do I need to buy any equipment?

No — and that's exactly the point. Deep Chill is a fully managed rental service. You don't need to spend £1,000+ on a setup — we provide everything for you. We deliver, install, and maintain your cold plunge so you can simply use it without any hassle or ongoing responsibility.

Do I need to add ice?

No — there's no need to add ice at all. The system keeps the water at your chosen temperature automatically, so you don't have to worry about buying ice, storing it, or managing the temperature yourself. Everything is handled for you.

How does it work?

We deliver and install a cold plunge tub along with a chiller at your home. The chiller continuously circulates the water and maintains it at a consistent low temperature, so it is always ready when you are. You can use it whenever you like, and we return regularly to clean, maintain, and fully refresh the water.

What are the benefits of cold plunging?

Cold plunging is known to support a wide range of physical and mental benefits. These include reduced inflammation and muscle soreness, faster recovery after exercise, pain relief and reduced stiffness, increased energy levels, improved focus and mental clarity, and better stress resilience. It may also help support mental wellbeing and reduce feelings of anxiety when used regularly as part of a healthy routine. Many people find that even a short session leaves them feeling more alert, calm, and refreshed.

How long before I see benefits?

Many people notice immediate effects such as increased alertness and energy after their first few sessions. With regular use, typically a few times per week, longer-term benefits such as improved recovery, resilience, and overall wellbeing are often noticed within a few weeks. Consistency is key.

What temperature should the water be?

We recommend around 8°C for the best balance of effectiveness and consistency. This temperature provides strong cold exposure benefits while also helping to maintain optimal water conditions between service visits. If you are new to cold plunging, you can start slightly warmer and gradually reduce the temperature over time.

How long should I stay in?

For beginners, 1–3 minutes is usually enough. More experienced users may stay in for 3–5 minutes. You don't need long sessions — consistency and regular use are more important than duration. As a general guideline, research suggests that around 11 minutes per week in total, spread across multiple sessions, can be enough to experience the benefits of cold exposure.

How often do I need to use it to see benefits?

You don't need to spend a long time in the cold. Research suggests that around 11 minutes per week, spread across a few short sessions, can be enough to experience benefits. Most customers use their cold plunge 3–5 times per week for a few minutes at a time.

What time of day is best?

The best time depends on your routine and goals. Morning sessions are great for boosting energy and focus, while using it after exercise can help with recovery. Some people also use it in the evening to unwind, although it can feel energising, so earlier in the evening is usually preferred. The most important factor is consistency.

How much electricity does it use?

The cooler uses electricity in a similar way to a small fridge, cycling on and off to maintain the set temperature rather than running constantly. In most home setups, the running cost is typically around £10–£20 per month, depending on outdoor temperature, placement, and usage.

Will this increase my water bill?

There is a small increase, but it is very minimal. We fully refresh the water during our maintenance visits to ensure high hygiene standards. This typically uses around 900–1,200 litres per month, which usually costs around £2–£4. Most customers do not notice any meaningful difference in their water bill.

Do you add any chemicals to the water?

No — we use fresh tap water only, with no chemicals added. The water is continuously circulated through the system to help keep it clean between visits, and we fully refresh the water during our regular maintenance visits.

Do I need to manage the water?

No. With our full-service plan, we handle all cleaning, maintenance, and water management for you. You don't need to worry about anything — it's all taken care of.

What happens to the water when it is drained?

As part of our regular maintenance, we fully drain and refresh the water to maintain high hygiene standards. The water is safely discharged into a suitable outdoor drain or designated area, depending on your setup. We take care to ensure this is done responsibly and without any disruption to your property.

Do you clean the tub on-site?

No — to maintain the highest hygiene standards, we don't clean the tub on-site. Instead, we replace it with a freshly cleaned unit and take the used one away for a full off-site clean. This approach ensures everything is handled professionally, with no mess or disruption at your home.

Where can it be installed?

The system can be installed in your garden, garage, or indoors if you have a suitable space. We will assess your setup during installation and advise on the best and safest location.

Should I place it in sun or shade?

A shaded or partially shaded area is usually best, as it helps maintain a consistent temperature more efficiently. We will help you choose the ideal position based on your space.

Will the cover blow off in the wind?

As long as the cover is securely fastened after each use, this should not be an issue. We will show you exactly how to secure it properly during installation.

If the water is naturally colder than the set temperature, will it be heated?

No — the system is designed for cooling only. If the water is already colder due to outdoor conditions, the chiller will simply remain inactive until it needs to cool again.

Does the chiller make noise?

The chiller produces a low, steady sound similar to a small fridge or air conditioning unit. Most customers find it unobtrusive, especially when installed outdoors or in a garage. We will also help position it to minimise any impact.

What happens if I go on holiday?

No problem — just let us know in advance. We can either pause your service or adjust your maintenance schedule to suit your plans. We will work with you to ensure everything is handled smoothly while you are away.

What areas do you cover?

We mainly operate in Horley, Crawley, and Reigate, covering most parts of Surrey and West Sussex. This allows us to provide a reliable and responsive service while keeping travel times practical. If you are unsure whether we cover your location, simply send us your postcode and we will be happy to confirm. In some cases, we may still be able to accommodate locations slightly outside our core area depending on availability.

What happens if something breaks?

We take care of it. If anything goes wrong, we will repair or replace the equipment as part of your service, so you are never left dealing with issues yourself.

Can I try it before committing?

Yes — you can start with a short-term commitment and see how it works for you. Most customers continue once they experience the benefits.

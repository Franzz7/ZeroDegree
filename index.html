Common Questions

Quick answers.

Do I need to buy ice?

No. The chiller unit keeps the water cold automatically. You never need to buy or handle ice.

How often is it cleaned and serviced?

We visit every 10 days to clean the tub, check the chiller, and ensure everything is working correctly.

What is the minimum hire period?

The minimum hire period is 2 months. After that, you can extend month by month or arrange a collection.

What do I need at my property?

An outdoor power supply, an outdoor tap, a suitable level outdoor space, and safe access for our team. We confirm suitability before installation.

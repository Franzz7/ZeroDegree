Safety First

Please read before use.

Cold water immersion is not suitable for everyone. Please seek medical advice from your GP or doctor before starting cold plunge use if you have any of the following conditions or circumstances.

Who should consult a doctor before use?
You should speak to a doctor before using a cold plunge if you have or have had any of the following:

— Heart conditions or cardiovascular disease
— High or low blood pressure
— Raynaud's disease or other circulatory conditions
— Respiratory conditions
— Neurological conditions
— Pregnancy
— Recent surgery or injury
— Any condition for which cold or physical shock may pose a risk

If you are unsure whether cold water immersion is suitable for you, consult a qualified medical professional before starting.
General safety guidance
— Start with shorter sessions at higher temperatures and gradually build up as you become accustomed
— Do not plunge alone until you are fully familiar with how your body responds
— Keep the plunge area free of trip hazards and ensure you can exit safely at all times
— Do not plunge after heavy alcohol consumption
— If you feel unwell, faint, or experience chest pain at any point — exit the water immediately
— Never leave children unsupervised near the tub

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything else.

How cold does it get?

The chiller allows you to set your preferred temperature, typically ranging from around 3°C to 15°C. We'll help you set the right temperature on installation day, and you can adjust it from there.

Do I need to buy ice?

No. The chiller unit maintains the water temperature automatically. You never need to buy, store, or handle ice at any point.

How often is it cleaned and serviced?

We visit every 10 days to clean the tub, treat the water, check the chiller, inspect all equipment, and top up the water level if needed. You don't need to do any of this yourself.

What is included in the monthly price?

The monthly hire fee includes the cold plunge tub, the chiller unit, delivery and installation, all 10-day service visits, water treatment during visits, and ongoing support. See our pricing page for full details.

How long is the minimum hire period?

The minimum hire period is 2 months. After that, the hire continues on a rolling monthly basis — you can extend as long as you like or arrange a return collection with reasonable notice.

What do I need at my property?

An outdoor power socket (13A), an outdoor tap, a level stable surface for the tub (patio, decking, concrete), and clear access to the installation area. Full details on our requirements page.

Can I move the tub after it's been installed?

We ask that you don't move the tub or chiller yourself after installation. If you need it repositioned for any reason, contact us and we can arrange this during a service visit or separately. Moving the equipment incorrectly could damage it or affect water chemistry.

Is it suitable for beginners?

Yes. The temperature is adjustable, so you can start warmer and work your way colder as your body adapts. Most people find a starting point of 12°C–15°C manageable. We'd always recommend starting with shorter sessions and building from there. Please read the safety section above before starting.

What happens if I want to keep it longer?

After the minimum 2-month hire, you can simply carry on. The hire rolls over month by month and you'll be billed monthly. No new contract is needed — just let us know if you decide to return it, and we'll arrange collection with reasonable notice.

What happens if something goes wrong with the equipment?

Contact us directly and we'll respond as quickly as possible. Minor faults will be resolved at your next scheduled service visit. Anything more urgent we'll deal with as a priority. The equipment is covered under our responsibility during the hire period for normal use.

How do I set or adjust the temperature?

The chiller unit has a simple control panel. We'll show you how to use it on installation day. Temperature adjustments are straightforward and take effect gradually over a few hours.

What areas do you cover?

We currently serve Surrey and surrounding areas. If you're not sure whether we cover your postcode, include it in your enquiry and we'll confirm.

What happens at the end of my hire?

We arrange a collection visit, drain and remove the tub and chiller, and leave the area tidy. There's no charge for collection. Your refundable deposit is returned following inspection of the equipment.

Can I purchase the equipment after hire?

We may offer a purchase option in the future. If and when that becomes available, existing hire customers will hear about it first. There's no guaranteed purchase option at this stage.

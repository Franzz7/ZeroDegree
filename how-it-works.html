The Process

Simple from
day one.

Five steps from first contact to cold water — and we handle most of them.

01

Enquire

Fill in our short form and tell us about your home. No commitment — we just need enough to check availability and suitability.

No obligation Quick response Short form
02

We check your setup

Before anything is booked, we confirm your property is suitable. Four things are needed — we'll let you know clearly if anything's missing.

Outdoor power socket Outdoor tap Level outdoor space Clear access
03

Delivery & installation

We arrive with everything. Tub positioned, chiller connected, water filled and running — we walk you through it before we leave. Most installs take a few hours.

We bring everything Full setup included Ready same day
04

We service it every 10 days

Every 10 days we visit, clean the tub, check the chiller, and treat the water. You don't need to be home — and you don't need to do a thing.

Tub clean Water treatment Chiller check No action needed from you
05

Renew or return

Minimum hire is 2 months. After that, continue month by month for as long as you like — or arrange a collection. We remove everything and leave the area tidy.

2-month minimum Month-to-month after Collection included
Something goes wrong?

Contact us directly. We respond quickly and deal with anything urgent as a priority. Equipment faults from normal use are on us — not you.
